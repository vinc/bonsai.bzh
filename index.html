Gwezennig

Bonsai Nursery

Gwezennig is a Breton word that means "small tree" in English, which is fitting for a small scale bonsai nursery.

Our goal is to select and grow young trees that have the best genetics for bonsai from a selection of Japanese and European species.

Japanese Species

  • Acer buergerianum, Trident maple, Toukaede (トウカエデ)
  • Acer palmatum, Japanese maple, Momiji (イロハモミジ)
    • Deshojo (出猩猩)
    • Katsura (桂)
    • Kiyohime (清姬)
  • Chaenomeles japonica, Japanese quince, Kusaboke (クサボケ)
    • Chojubai (長寿梅)
  • Juniperus chinensis, Chinese juniper, Shimpaku (真柏)
    • Itoigawa (糸魚川)
    • Kishu (纪州)
  • Pinus thunbergii, Japanese black pine, Kuromatsu (黒松)
  • Pseudocydonia sinensis, Chinese quince, Karin (花梨)
  • Zelkova serrata, Japanese elm, Keyaki (欅)

European Species

  • Carpinus betulus, Hornbeam
  • Crataegus monogyna, Hawthorn
  • Prunus spinosa, Blackthorn
